The story of how Maggie Moo’s came to be is a quite interesting one. The first one opened in 1989 in Kansas City, Kansas. Kansas’ residents, Juel and Katherine Tillery, decided to open an ice cream store. The only problem was coming up with a suitable name. Then one day it hit them! Their young daughter called a cow, on a farm they passed every day, “MaggieMoo”, little did they know this was the start of something great. Currently there are 19 locations and 400 stores opened in the United States.
